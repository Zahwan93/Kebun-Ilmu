B. Example Conversation (Contoh Percakapan)

Percakapan dua teman yang sedang makan bakso:

A: What are you eating?

B: I’m eating bakso.

A: Oh, it smells so good!

B: Yes, it is! What delicious bakso!

A: Where did you buy it?

B: At the street food vendor near the school.

A: Kamu sedang makan apa?

B: Aku sedang makan bakso.

A: Wah, baunya enak sekali!

B: Iya! Baksonya enak sekali!

A: Kamu beli di mana?

B: Di penjual kaki lima dekat sekolah.

C. Example Narrative (Contoh Narasi Pendek)

Teks narasi tentang pengalaman makan bakso:

Yesterday, I went to a small food stall with my friends. We were very hungry after playing football.

I ordered a bowl of bakso. It was hot, spicy, and very tasty. The meatballs were big and soft. I added noodles, fried onions, and chili sauce.

I said, “What delicious bakso!” and my friends agreed. It was my favorite meal that day.

Kemarin, saya pergi ke warung kecil bersama teman-teman. Kami sangat lapar setelah bermain sepak bola.

Saya memesan semangkuk bakso. Baksonya panas, pedas, dan sangat enak. Bola dagingnya besar dan empuk. Saya menambahkan mie, bawang goreng, dan sambal.

Saya berkata, “Baksonya enak banget!” dan teman-teman saya setuju. Itu adalah makanan favorit saya hari itu.
